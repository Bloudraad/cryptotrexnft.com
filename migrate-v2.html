Let's migrate!

  • Change your duplicate T-rex into a whole new T-rex.
  • Assign correct properties to your T-rexes.
  • Send you free 10 $FOSSIL tokens for each T-rex migrated.

Follow this 3 basic steps to migrate your CryptoTrexes

  1. Sign In, metamask will prompt you to sign and connect the CryptoTrex website to your address.
  2. Approve, before we begin migrating, we would need to approve the migration action. This is Ethereum's security measure to make sure that the next transaction is not malicious.
  3. Migrate, finally migrate your CryptoTrex by migrating one-by-one or by pressing the "Migrate All" button to save gas if you have multiple Rexes.
